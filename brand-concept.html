Tourism Brand Concept

Branding Strategy Design

Project Overview

This brand concept was developed to reimagine local tourism experiences. The core objective was to create a visual identity and strategy that feels authentic, modern, and deeply rooted in the local culture, while appealing to international tourists looking for immersive travel.

The Challenge

Many local markets and tourism spots suffer from outdated branding that fails to communicate the rich heritage and vibrant energy of the destination. The challenge was to bridge the gap between traditional culture and modern aesthetics.

The Solution

By conducting extensive market research and studying tourist behavior, a new brand language was formed. This included a minimalist yet expressive logo, a curated monochrome and earth-tone color palette, and a comprehensive marketing strategy aimed at social media engagement and eco-conscious travel.
